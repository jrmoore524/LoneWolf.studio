Light in Darkness

The Pharaoh of the city is guided by the tweleve crystal skulls, and to get his people to build a tower to heaven, the Tower of Babel. The skulls also tell the Pharaoh that he needs to find the thirteenth skull to finish the tower. The skull was found, and the tower is finished, but when the Pharaoh climbs the tower with the thirteenth skull, the aliens under the direction of God make all the people speak different languages, and they start quarreling. God says, "I am the light", and then knocks down the tower. From then on the world is chaotic and war-torn. The aliens then are instruction by God to scatter the now cursed crystal skulls all across the world. If they are ever brought together again, the gates of Hell will be opened.
